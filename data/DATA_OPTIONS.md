3.2 Data Standards
Market dataset must be sourced by your team, typically:
- Equity price data (daily)
This dataset is used for:
- Underlying spot price
- Volatility emission
- Risk metrics (VaR, scenarios)

3.3 Acceptable Data Downloads
Public APIs (Reccomended):
- Yahoo Finance (yfinance)
- Alpha Vintage
- Financial Modelling Prep
- FRED (macro/rates only)

Institutional Sources (Optional):
- Bloomberg (export to CSV)

Manual Downloads (Acceptable if Documented):
- ASX
- Yahoo Finance Website
- RBA Tables
